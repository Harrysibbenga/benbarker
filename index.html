About Benjamin Barker

Age :

27

Height :

6ft4

Born :

Cambridge, UK

Resides :

London, UK

Enjoy :

Surfing, Rock climbing, the outdoors!

I am a professional racing driver currently competing in the FIA World Endurance Championship. I race for Gulf Racing driving the #86 Porsche RSR in GTEAM. I have had success world wide winning the Australian Formula 3 championship, x3 Bathurst 12hr champion, Vice champion Porsche Carrera Cup GB and a lap record holder at Le Mans 24hr. Motorsport has been in my blood from day 1 with my mother guiding me into the industry through her racing enthusiasm.. Thanks Mum!

Along side my own racing I help other drivers improve with 'one to one’ on-track tuition. As well as career guidance for amateur level and professional level drivers. I find it hugely satisfying sharing my knowledge and experience and seeing people take more enjoyment out of motorsport through improvement.
